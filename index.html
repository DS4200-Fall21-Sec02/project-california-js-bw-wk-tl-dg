Abstract

When considering where to live, there are a few common factors that are essential to understand before coming to a decision. This visualization aims to provide users with a way to assess how easy it will be for them to live in different areas of California. The visualization will provide a representation of the average rent price, salary range, weather, and crime level for the different counties in California, as well as an adjusted ‘master score’ to represent the ‘most livable’ areas, that we define as having higher salaries, lower rent costs, lower crime rates, and more moderate weather.
